Military-Grade File Protection & Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Experience unparalleled protection with our advanced security architecture, combining zero-knowledge encryption protocols and AES-GCM encryption to safeguard your sensitive information and credentials.

  • End-to-End Encryption - Data is encrypted on your device before transmission, ensuring privacy throughout its journey.

  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture - Only you possess the keys to decrypt your data; we have no access to your unencrypted information.

  • AES-256 - Utilized by governments and financial institutions, AES-256 offers top-tier security.

  • Galois/Counter Mode (GCM) - Provides authenticated encryption, ensuring data integrity and confidentiality.

  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) - Adds an extra layer of security to your account access.

  • Open Source Transparency - Our codebase is publicly available, allowing for community-driven security audits and trust.

  const iv = crypto.getRandomValues(new Uint8Array(12));
  const encryptionKey = await deriveKey(userKey);
  
  const encryptedData = await crypto.subtle.encrypt(
    { name: 'AES-GCM', iv },
    encryptionKey,
    new TextEncoder().encode(JSON.stringify(data))
  );
  
  return {
    iv: arrayBufferToBase64(iv),

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our zero-knowledge password manager and encrypted file storage

What is zero-knowledge encryption and how does it protect my data?

Zero-knowledge encryption means that only you have access to your encryption keys. We cannot see, access, or recover your passwords, files, or any other data stored in your vault. Your data is encrypted on your device before it reaches our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Is Zecrypt really open source and free to use?

Yes, Zecrypt is completely open source and free forever. Our source code is publicly available on GitHub, allowing security experts and developers to audit our encryption methods and security practices. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers.

What types of files and credentials can I store securely?

You can store passwords, API keys, credit card information, SSH keys, Wi-Fi credentials, software licenses, personal notes, and any confidential documents. Our military-grade AES-256 encryption protects all your sensitive data with the same level of security used by banks and government agencies.

How does Zecrypt compare to other password managers?

Unlike many password managers, Zecrypt is completely open source, uses zero-knowledge architecture, and offers encrypted file storage alongside password management. We don't store your data in plain text, and our code is transparent and auditable by the security community.

Can I access my encrypted vault from multiple devices?

Yes, Zecrypt provides cross-platform access to your encrypted vault. You can securely access your passwords and files from any device with an internet connection. Your data remains encrypted in transit and at rest, ensuring security across all platforms.

What happens if I forget my master password?

Due to our zero-knowledge architecture, we cannot recover your master password or reset it for you. This is by design to ensure maximum security. We recommend using a strong, memorable master password and storing a backup of your recovery phrase in a secure location.
